## Icons

SVG icons are available in this `brand` directory. They hsould be copied to the correct location as needed.

All icons are provided as SVGs. They can be converted to other formats as needed.

The `NMPBX_NMPBX_*` are landscape orientation. They feature a stylised version of the product name "NMPBX". `Black_Logo` and `Main_Logo` need to be on a light background. `White_and_Green_Logo` and `White_Logo` need a dark background.

The `NMPBX_X_Isolated_*` are square. They feature just the "X" which is the primary logo. If the image is going to be displayed at a size of approximately 26px or smaller, `NMPBX_X_Isolated_Small.svg` should be used.

## Colours

`#0e2826` (dark green) used for background of title areas and menus. Use this in place of Linphone's default bright orange backgrounds.

`#25af4b` (light green) used as background for calls to action such as the button to start a call. Avoid having multiple buttons with this colour visible at once. Also used for when a user's status is 'Available' (to avoid having two similar but slightly different greens on screen at once.)

## Typeface

Use Poppins for titles and body text.

The truetype font files are available in the `Poppins` sub-directory of this `brand` directory. They should be copied to the correct location as needed.

Bold for main titles.

Medium for sub-titles.

Light for body text.
